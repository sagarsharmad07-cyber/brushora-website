About the artist

Drawn slowly, made personally.

BRUSHORA is built for an independent artist who works across charcoal, graphite, ink, gouache, and acrylic washes. The style is expressive without losing the likeness of the subject.

Visitors can browse originals, request a personal portrait, or ask for custom work such as family drawings, pet portraits, gift pieces, and memorial artwork.
